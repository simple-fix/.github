# Code of Conduct

Most codes of conduct are long enough to need their own terms of service. Here's the short version:

**Be decent.** Show empathy, give constructive feedback, and treat others like humans—not abstractions on a screen.

**Don't be a jerk.** No harassment, no trolling, no doxxing. If you wouldn't say it to someone's face in a room full of their friends, don't say it here.

**If something's off, say something.** Email **conduct@simple-fix.site**. I'll look into it promptly and fairly. No bureaucracy, no committees—just a human on the other end who takes this seriously.

This applies everywhere under the simple-fix org: issues, PRs, discussions, and any official space.
